About us

With 50 years of experience in the promotions imprinting world. We invite you to continue to call upon Decorative Services for your imprinting needs.

We offer color matching, in-house plate making and graphics.

From our offices in Oceanside, California, we provide pad printing, screen printing, debossing, and laser engraving services, in a wide range of industries, including sporting goods, electronics, plastics, medical devices, automotive, appliances, and many more. Fine line, single, or multi-color pad printing. Short term or high volume production runs, let Decorative Services handle your printing needs.

Services

As a full service, on-site imprinting company, we offer a variety of decorative services. In addition to pad printing, silk screening and foil stamping, we have a fully capable graphic department. We can develop new art, revise old art or update existing art. We have a reputation of excellent customer service.

There are several factors that determine the type of service adequate for your needs; such as product size, surface, image size, imprint location and number of colors to be imprinted. To learn more about the services offered and see some sample pictures please click on the links below.

Pad Printing is a very versatile method of imprinting. Concave and convex surfaces are easily imprinted with this method, with virtually any substrate.

Pad Printing

Laser engraving produces a mark that is crisp, clean and permanent. Among products that can be engraved are: wood/natural materials, plastics, metals, glass, and stone.

Laser Engraving

Screen printing can be used for a large variety of substrates. Including painted surfaces, metal, plastic, and paper. Screen printing is best suited for imprinting on flat surfaces.

Screen printing

Foil Stamping provides the best visual appearance for metallics of all shades. The best substrates for foil stamping are plastics, paper goods, vinyl, leather and leatherette.
